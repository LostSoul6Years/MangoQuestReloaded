What is MangoQuestReloaded? 什麽是芒果任務重製版?

MangoQuestReloaded is a miraculous quest plugin which is capable of reenacting your old RPG soul with a plain book interface,catering to old rpg lovers. It features a very easy to use book GUI for both gameplay and administrative work. Its predecessor, MangoQuest, originally written by Cutiemango with the great purpose of serving all RPG-purposes servers but it was unfortunate that the plugin had to come to an end with the disappearance of the original author. This plugin was revamped and forked to become MangoquestReloaded by SakurajiKanade to deliver more powerful updates and regular maintenance, despite it have already become considerably dead.

MangoQuestReloaded 是一個神奇的任務插件，能夠通過簡單的書本界面重現你的舊 RPG 靈魂，迎合老 rpg 愛好者。 它具有非常易於使用的書籍 GUI，可用於遊戲和管理工作。 它的前身 MangoQuest 最初由 Cutiemango 編寫，其偉大目的是為所有 RPG 用途的服務器提供服務，但不幸的是，該插件不得不隨著原作者的消失而告終。 這個插件被 SakurajiKanade 改進並分叉為 MangoquestReloaded，以提供更強大的更新和定期維護，儘管它已經變得相當死了。
